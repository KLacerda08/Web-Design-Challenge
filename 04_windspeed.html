Wind Speed


Based on the data, the highest wind speeds were measured in the northern hemisphere at approximately 60 degrees north latitude. Consistent with with cloudiness and humidity, there was not linear relationship between cloudiness and distance from the Equator. However, there appear to be areas of relatively concentrated areas of higher wind speeds at the Equator, around 30 south latitude, and around 60 degrees north latitude. These results may be dependent on the measurement day and time of year, and could be related to other weather patterns.
