Humidity


Based on the data, while there does not appear to be a linear relationship between humidity an distance from the Equator, there appear to be areas of relatively concentrated high humidity at the Equator, and in the far northern latitudes. These results may be dependent on the measurement day and time of year. Additionally, one must consider the effect of temperature on relative humidity to understand its true effect.
